Foundation Property Maintenance Course

Our course is a multi-disciplinary programme which covers a range of property maintenance skills including repairing/maintaining walls and internal/external fixtures and fittings, plumbing, tiling, and painting and decorating.

No previous experience or qualifications are necessary. Our training areas have been specially designed to reflect a live working environment.

Course Breakdown

How long is the course?

The course is an intensive 5 week foundation course.

What will I learn?

Your training will include the following elements

  • Health safety and welfare in construction
  • Sustain ability in the construction Industry
  • Repair and maintain masonry structures
  • Maintain and repair internal and external fixtures and fittings
  • Painting and decorating
  • Domestic plumbing
  • Introduction to self-employment or
  • Building a personal career portfolio
  • CSCS training and examination

What are the qualifications?

The initial five week course is a non-accredited programme, although you will achieve a Level 1 Health and Safety in the Construction Environment and your Construction Skills Certification Scheme (CSCS) card, essential for working in the construction industry.

Our training can prepare you for a Level 2 or 3 qualification in multi-trades or a single discipline.

What happens after the course?

This will depend on your personal situation and your career ambitions. We aim to support you into a career in the building trades, whether that is as an employee or self-employed, and will provide guidance and advice appropriate to your needs.

Is the course residential?

Yes the course is residential. Monday–Friday with all food provided.

How long is a day’s training?

Your day will start at 8:30 am and will finish around 4pm. You may be required to study in the evenings and weekends.
